Facilities and amenities> Access
We aim to make access to the paintings enjoyable and welcoming to the widest possible public.
Cloakrooms
Cloakrooms cost £2 per item to use (free for Members). We accept bags up to 56 x 25 x 45 cm. You are welcome to bring baby buggies into the Gallery.
Restrooms
There are restrooms and baby changing facilities at the entrance and exit, and also in the Education Centre
Explore the collection
Explore the nation's collection before you visit through our app for information on the go.
WiFi
Connect to our free WiFi - look for ‘National Gallery’.
Photography
Feel free to take photographs for personal, non-commercial use, unless a sign says you shouldn't.
