Safety and hygiene A contactless experience
We are the same Gallery but with a reduced capacity, art routes to guide you, and a contactless experience.
Face coverings
Our staff will be wearing face coverings for their safety and the safety of our visitors, and we encourage you to do the same.
NHS Test and Trace
In line with government guidance, we encourage you to register your visit with NHS Test and Trace upon arrival by scanning the QR code or by giving your details to a member of staff.
Increased cleaning
The frequency of our cleaning throughout the Gallery has been increased, including toilets and other facilities.
Hand sanitiser stations
Hand sanitiser will be available, at regular intervals, for you to use.
If you're feeling unwell
If you or anyone you live with displays symptoms associated with COVID-19, please delay your visit until it is safe to do so.
